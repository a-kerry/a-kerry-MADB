The Creative Revolution: Unleashing AI's Impact on Video Projects


As artificial intelligence (AI) continues to progress, it has become increasingly integrated into creative endeavours like cinematography, music composition, photography, and literature. One standout AI tool in this realm is ChatGPT, boasting over 100 million users worldwide.

This Website intends to introduce AI tools to video content creators, presenting the advantages, disadvantages, and future implications of such tools relative to creative video projects.
As well as providing additional resources to allow users to decide if AI tools are applicable to their content.

ADVANTAGES

Discussing advantages
Such as: content quality, automation and content inspiration.

DISADVANTAGES

Discussing disadvantages
Such as: dehumanisation of creativity, and AI's lack of human understanding.

FUTURE-IMPLICATIONS

Discussing the various future scenarios between AI and video content creation.
Such as: monopolisation of content creation and the potential for 'Human-Made' premium.

OTHER SOURCES

Provides viewers with various sources, to expand their knowledge of AI and AI tools.
