Disadvantages of AI tools


This Page aims to discuss and present the disadvantages of AI tools for creative video projects, such as Dehumanisation of creativity and AI's lack of human understanding.

Overview of the Disadvantages of AI tools for creative video projects


While AI offers new possibilities for creative expression, it also poses challenges to the unique qualities and personal experiences that human creators bring to their work. Where there is the possibility of related disadvantages such as the Dehumanisation of creativity



Dehumanisation of creativity

It is proposed that AI tools can dehumanise creativity by challenging traditional notions of human agency, expression, emotion, and labour involved in regular creative processes.

When AI creates art, it has the potential to overshadow human creators, raising questions about the authenticity and originality of video content created.

Furthermore, AI's cost-cutting abilities for creative companies, can threaten human jobs. An example of this is the Writers Guild of America’s strike, which demanded that motion picture and television studios safeguard their jobs from the use of AI.

Overall, while AI offers new possibilities, it also raises concerns about the role of human emotion and labour in the creative process.

AI's lack of human understanding

It is argued that AI tools lack a basic understanding of human values, socially and emotionally.
As AI lacks human experience and emotions that drive creative work, and provide resonance with audiences creating a connection between creator and viewer based upon shared experiences.

Therefore, AI tools can potentially lessen the role of human creators, raising questions over the authenticity of AI-generated projects.

Relative to content creators who employ AI tools for their video projects, this could result in the audience questioning the creator’s authenticity, affecting viewer-creator relationships.
